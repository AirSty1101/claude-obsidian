# Reinforcement Learning in Finance

**Reinforcement Learning in Finance** is the application of RL models (like [[Deep Q-Network (DQN)]] or PPO) to algorithmic trading and portfolio management. Instead of framing trading as a supervised learning anomaly (e.g., predicting the exact future price), RL treats it as a sequential decision-making problem. 

The agent learns to navigate a highly stochastic environment (the market) by taking discrete or continuous actions to maximize a custom reward function over time, directly optimizing for execution algorithms or portfolio allocation rather than just price forecasting accuracy.
