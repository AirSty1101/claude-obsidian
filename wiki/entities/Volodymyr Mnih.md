# Volodymyr Mnih

Volodymyr Mnih is an artificial intelligence researcher and the lead author of the seminal DeepMind papers introducing the [[Deep Q-Network (DQN)]], including the 2015 Nature publication "Human-level control through deep reinforcement learning." His work demonstrated that a single neural network could learn to master diverse complex environments directly from high-dimensional inputs, establishing the foundation for modern deep reinforcement learning applications.
